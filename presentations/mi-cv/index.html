Research Experience

Internship @ CHU Grenoble

  • 🗓️ From April to July 2021
  • 🥅 We wanted to know if the trajectories of tacrolimus blood level concentration affected kidney allograft survival
  • 🐍 We used python, R and automatic clustering based on Dynamic Time Warping to determine 3 patient groups
  • 😥 Unfortunately, no link to survival was found :(

Researcher @ IARC / WHO


  • 🗓️ From February 2022 to January 2023
  • 🌐 We developed CanGraph, a python utility to study and analyse cancer-associated metabolites using knowledge graphs
  • 📥 When given a query, the program searches 7 existing, high quality databases, and returns a GraphML export of related information
  • 📜 We want to publish the results, which led to my Master's Thesis, to a big Journal, such as Bioinformatics or Scientific Reports

Internship @ Turing Biosystems


  • 😎 My Current Job!
  • At Turing Bio, we work in the intersection of Academia 🧪 and Enterprise 🏬 to elect, optimize, and design new therapies based on existing data
  • 👀 Based on the work done at IARC, I am transforming databases to provide as input to Turing's Interpretable AI Platform

Additional Experience
